About:

Phoenix park Florist is in business since November 1999, We have moved to a new location to make it easier on our customers with parking etc... We supply flowers for all occassions and can send flowers worldwide as a full gold member of Interflora. On special orders we can add chocolates, teddies or Balloons, Champagne or wine can be organised. We also have a wide range of seasonal gifts christmas, Easter and Halloween. Two-experienced florists are here to answer any questions you may have. Our new address is 129 Thomas St, The Liberties, Dublin, D08 F6W3
